ROCK ARENA

Throw rocks • Dodge everything • Last ball standing

Private Lobby

Enter a name · Names are globally unique · Press Esc in-game to view players · Mobile: touch to play

Controls — PC

W A S D Move (relative to where you're looking)
Mouse Look around / aim — click canvas to capture mouse
Left click Throw a rock (6 ammo max, refills over time)
Space Dash in your movement direction (3s cooldown)
Q Raise shield — deflects rocks & blocks Laser Beam
E Heal — restore 10 HP instantly (6s cooldown)
R Shockwave — blast nearby players away (12s cooldown)
F Hold to charge Laser Beam — fires after 1.5s, pierces players
Esc Open player list / leave game

Controls — Mobile

  • Left side joystick to move; right side swipe to look around
  • SHOOT (big red) — throw rock  |  DASH — bottom right corner
  • HEAL, LASER, SHIELD, SHOCK — radial buttons around SHOOT
  • Touch anywhere to go full screen

Lobbies

  • FFA — Free for all. Kill everyone, survive as long as you can
  • Team Duels — 🔴 Red vs 🔵 Blue teams. No friendly fire. First team to 10 kills wins the round. Names show in team colour
  • Hot Potato 🥔 — One player has the potato (gold name + aura). Only the holder can shoot. Throw the potato rock to pass it. If the timer runs out… you EXPLODE! Shockwave & dash still work. No shield, heal, or laser.
  • Up to 10 players per lobby  ·  Respawn in 3s after death
  • Walk into the purple portal (top of map) to return to lobby

Private Lobbies

  • Type a code name, pick a gamemode, then click Host. Share the code with friends
  • Friends type the same code → click Join to enter your lobby
  • If the code is already taken: pick another. Empty lobbies close automatically
  • Max 10 players per private lobby

Hats & Coins

  • Earn coins by completing quests in the Codes tab (harder quests = bigger rewards)
  • Redeem codes (found in GitHub Releases) for instant coins
  • Buy and equip hats in the Shop tab — they appear on your character in-game
  • Progress (coins, hats) saves automatically in your browser

Rules & Tips

  • Each rock hit deals 10 damage; Laser Beam deals 30 and pierces multiple players
  • Rocks bounce off walls & pillars (up to 4 bounces) — gold glow = bounced rock
  • Shield deflects rocks back and blocks Laser Beam from the front
  • 3-kill streak → meteor shower (3s warning) — you are immune to your own meteors
  • Press Esc to open the player list
  • Your name is globally unique — no two players anywhere can share the same name
