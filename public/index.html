ROCK ARENA

Throw rocks • Dodge everything • Last ball standing

Move mouse to look around  ·  WASD move  ·  Right-click throw  ·  F Kamehameha  ·  Q shield  ·  Space dash

Controls

W A S D Move around
Mouse Look around / aim
Right click Throw a rock (10 ammo max, refills over time)
Space Dash in your movement direction (3s cooldown)
Q Activate shield — blocks the next hit (8s cooldown)
F Hold to charge Kamehameha laser — releases after 1.5s

Rules

  • Each player starts with 50 HP
  • Each rock hit deals 10 damage
  • Kamehameha laser deals 30 damage and passes through players
  • Rocks bounce off walls and cover rocks (up to 4 bounces)
  • Shield absorbs one hit completely — even Kamehameha
  • Last player alive wins the round
  • Up to 10 players per lobby across 3 lobbies

Tips

  • Use cover rocks to dodge — hide and peek out to throw
  • Bounced rocks glow gold and are harder to dodge
  • Save your shield for Kamehameha — it blocks it completely
  • Dash to escape tight situations or close the gap fast
  • The minimap (bottom right) shows all living players
