ROCK ARENA

Throw rocks • Dodge everything • Last ball standing

WASD move  ·  Mouse aim  ·  Right-click throw  ·  Space dash  ·  Q shield  ·  E heal  ·  R shockwave  ·  F Kamehameha

Controls

W A S D Move (relative to where you're looking)
Mouse Look around / aim (horizontal + vertical) — click to capture mouse
Right click Throw a rock (10 ammo max, refills over time)
Space Dash in your movement direction (3s cooldown)
Q Raise physical shield — deflects rocks back & blocks Kamehameha
E Heal — restore 10 HP instantly (6s cooldown)
R Shockwave — blasts nearby players away from you (no damage, 12s cooldown)
F Hold to charge Kamehameha laser — releases after 1.5s

Rules

  • Each player starts with 75 HP — game is always ongoing, respawn in 3s after death
  • Each rock hit deals 10 damage; Kamehameha deals 30 and pierces multiple players
  • Rocks bounce off walls and pillars (up to 4 bounces) — gold glow = bounced rock
  • Shield deflects rocks back at the thrower and blocks Kamehameha completely
  • 6 ammo max — refills automatically over time
  • Get a 3-kill streak to call a meteor shower (3s warning before impact) — you are immune
  • Walk into the purple portal (top of map) to return to lobby any time
  • Up to 10 players per lobby across 3 always-live lobbies

Tips

  • Grind your kill streak: 3 kills → meteor warning → 3s later fire rains down
  • Shield deflects rocks back — bait an enemy throw then raise your shield
  • Look up/down with the mouse to aim Kamehameha and rocks at different heights
  • Dash to escape tight situations or close gaps fast
  • Compass at the top helps navigate — portal is always to the North
  • The portal shows on the minimap in the bottom-right corner
